December'sFeatured Artist

SCRATCH teamed up with Emily Wagner, artist, designer, and Editor in Chief of LA’s hottest beauty/fitness/wellness blog Groomed L.A., to create a holiday nail wrap collection that is the perfect blend of classic and cutting edge.

An accomplished fine-artist, designer, and illustrator, Emily creates fabric and textile designs for home décor and apparel that can be found in shops including Rory Beca, Forever 21, and Ten Over Six.

Emily treats each piece of fabric as a painting, and follows the same process for creating textiles that goes into producing an art piece. Her process is an elaborate one of pushing and pulling, placing and removing of color, texture and composition until the right balance (or imbalance) is achieved.

Applying these same principles to this collaboration, Emily designed this collection to cover all of your wintry nail art dreams.
